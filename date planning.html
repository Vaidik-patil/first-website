Option 1: Dinner and a Movie

One classic date option is to go out for dinner and then see a movie. Choose a restaurant and a movie that you both will enjoy.

Option 2: Outdoor Adventure

Take advantage of the great outdoors and go for a hike, a bike ride, or a picnic. Pack a picnic basket and head to a nearby park or nature reserve.

Option 3: Cultural Excursion

Take in some culture by visiting a museum, art gallery, or historical site. This is a great way to learn something new and spark interesting conversation.

Option 4: Cooking Class

Learn a new skill together by taking a cooking class. This is a fun and interactive date option that will also give you a delicious meal to enjoy together.
